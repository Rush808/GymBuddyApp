The bench press is the one of the most popular workouts in the gym. This workout prioritizes the middle and the upper part of your chest. Having this exercise in your workout can grant you a bigger and muscular chest.

  • Lie on your back while you bring your chest up on a flat bench.
  • Align your eyes with the bar.
  • Grasp the bar and plant your feet firmly into the ground.
  • Slowly lift the bar off the rack and lower the bar slowly and let it touch just right below your nipple.
  • Then, press your feet into the floow and push the bar back up.
